Four calls
150 pts
4 calls of any type (straight/triplet/quad).
hand must be ????p, where ?=T/S.
Example:
[123b][RRRR][345c][456c]7d + 7d

Twin Straight
100 pts
Two Straight with identical suit and number.
hand must be SS??p.
Example:
[123c][123c]222d4455d + 5d

Double Twin Straight
Overrides [Twin Straight]
When [Twin Straight] happens twice in a single hand.
hand must be SSSSp. if explained using other approaches, then this Fan is not possible (such as concealed hand can be explained as seven pairs)
Example:
223344778899c N + N*

334455c1236678d + 6d

Simple hand

Win After a Quad
500 pts
Overrides Win by Self-Drawn.
Win after the Quad, and there's no redraw between the quad and the win.
Hand must be T???p.
Marked with "+AQ" being one of the additional notes at the end of input.
Can create illegal input if the winning hand does not have a quad, or mistakenly marked as win by discard.
Example:
[234c][7777c] 33355b RR + R* +AQ

Robbing a Quad
500 pts
Win when someone is upgrading a triplet to a quad, and you can win with that tile.
Hand must be S???p.
Marked with "+RQ" being one of the additional notes at the end of input.
Can create illegal input if 5th (or more) of the same tile might be involved, or mistakenly marked as win by self drawn.
Example:
23345789c 666d RR +1c +RQ
Wrong Example:
2b 2233668899c 11d + 2b + RQ
For Robbing a Quad to happen, someone else need to get all 4x 2b before you win. So 2b should not appear in the first 13 tiles.

Last Tile Win / Grab the Moon
500 pts
Win on the final turn , where the entire tile wall was drawn.
In case of Win by Self-Drawn, Overrides Win by Self-Drawn.
Can be win by self drawn, or from other's discard.
Marked with "+LT" or "+GtM" being one of the additional notes at the end of input.
